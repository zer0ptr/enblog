WHO AM I?

Hi, My name is Hailin Zheng, a high school sophomore interested in information security. I spend my time on IoT security, Linux kernel exploitation, and AI safety—particularly risks in LLM-generated code, adversarial attacks, and skill poisoning. I also write a blog to track what I learn and share my research. Currently I’m working on a paper titled “Is Alignment Lost in Translation? Code Safety Across English and Chinese“ with the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Feel free to reach out if you’d like to talk.
